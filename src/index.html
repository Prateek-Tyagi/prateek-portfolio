About

Platforms as code, reliability as a habit

Platform engineer with 6+ years across DevOps and platform work, now leading observability and reliability at Safeguard Global for 30+ internal teams on a multi-account AWS organisation that runs payroll and workforce systems under SOC2 and CIS compliance. My work is the boring-in-a-good-way kind: self-service access without long-lived keys, monitors defined as code, supply-chain provenance checked before anything ships, and incidents that page the right team instead of everyone. I care about giving other engineers paved roads — and about the day the auditors show up going quietly.
